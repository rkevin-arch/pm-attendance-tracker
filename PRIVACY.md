# Privacy Policy

Right now, this bot has no local storage at all. Any attendance tracking is done in-memory and nothing is retained. In the future, we may add features such as a proper database to keep track of users across multiple meetings, mostly to facilitate checking which members are in good standing. In that case, only your discord user ID and your display name are kept track of. We have no plans on retaining any more info than that as of now, and will notify members if that changes.
